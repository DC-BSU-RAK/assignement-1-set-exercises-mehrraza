Stranger Things

Overview

Stranger Things is an American science fiction horror drama television series created by the Duffer Brothers. Set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, the show follows a group of kids who encounter supernatural forces and secret government experiments.

Plot

The first season begins in November 1983 when Will Byers disappears under strange circumstances. His friends, family, and the local police are drawn into a mystery involving top-secret government experiments, terrifying supernatural forces, and a strange little girl with psychokinetic abilities.

Main Cast

  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Gaten Matarazzo as Dustin Henderson
  • Caleb McLaughlin as Lucas Sinclair
